S.P.balasubrahmanyam

1946 - 2020

Biography

Sripathi Panditaradhyula Balasubrahmanyam (4 June 1946-25 September 2020),shortened as SPB,was an Indian playback singer,telivision presenter,actor,music composer,dubbing artist,and film producer.

He is widely regarded as one of the grateset Indian singers of all time. He predominantly worked in Telugu,tamil,kannada,Malayam, and Hindi films and sang in a total of 16 languuages.

Throughout his carrer,Balasubhramanyam has own awards not only for playback singing,but also for music direction,acting, and producing.He has won six Natioinal Film Awards for Best male Playback Singer.He was also awarded the Padma Shri in 2001 and the Padma Bhushan in 2011 and padma Vibhushan in 2021.
